Description

It’s Monday TTRPG Night at Screen Time arcade!

Everyone in the town of Fernweh knows and adores Sophia, the sweet co-owner of Screen Time arcade. Well, almost everyone. Sophia is tired of avoiding confrontation and keeping herself small. She wants to be a strong woman, but WHAT DOES THAT EVEN MEAN? 

You decide.

Play as Sophia, a trans Chinese American arcade owner, as she explores her identity via TTRPG games! Written with cultural consultants and sensitivity readers, R4C captures what it feels like to proudly declare who you are to the world, particularly if speaking softly is your normal. 

A love letter to the Toadhouse trans community and TTRPG enthusiasts. 
Playtime: A "one-shot" length of 60 minutes.


TTRPG and the Power of Games
Many people have used video games and TTRPG characters to experiment with different aspects of their self-expression. Roll for Confidence highlights how useful of a tool games can be, especially for the trans community. Sophia rolls various different characters and chats with her friends about their characters and what it feels like to be a trans woman, as she tries to figure out what feels best for her IRL.


Cultural Awareness
Roll for Confidence takes place in a fictional Northeast coastal town in the United States called Fernweh (pronounced Fern - Vee), with a strong LGBTQIA+ and diverse community. Toadhouse Games has reached out to the communities we aim to represent to ensure we do so in an accurate and respectful manner. We also hire cultural consultants and sensitivity readers.

CMC vignettes are unique snack sized games. They feature characters and locations from Call Me Cera. A portion of all Toadhouse Games sales goes to charities that benefit the marginalized groups our characters represent.
