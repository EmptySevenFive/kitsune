Description

In YEAR 2037, a pandemic of an unknown plague virus infection wiped out most of world animals and citizens. The world turns into a DEAD ZONE and survivors’ lives are threatened with zombies! The places you liked to hang out are now full of undead walking zombies and persons you know mostly disappear. Blood and deadly venom spreads everywhere... How will you survive in this Zombie Pandemic game?

DOUBTS & ANSWERS
Where do those persons and animals go? Think about where do these walking dead zombies come from? Find the answers, save the world!

KNOWLEDGE about DEAD ZOMBIES
Most zombies eat flesh and brain of living people, zombies differ in all ways: movement, damage, defense and attack pattern.

WEAPONS & ARMORS
Keep you defense up and improve your strength by using multiple specialized weapons and arm yourself with various armors! Always upgrade your weapons and equipment! A precise headshot can save you at the right time.

Game Features:
- Vivid battle effects with downwards angle
- Epic sound and music effects
- Challenging quests and missions on world maps
- Custom-designed weapons and undead creatures
- Story Mode and Survival Mode is alive, Multiplayer Mode is coming soon

TIPS FOR SURVIVORS
Stay calm and practice more
Claim daily rewards and win quest rewards
Spend your cash and coins properly
Use powerful weapons to shoot zombies
