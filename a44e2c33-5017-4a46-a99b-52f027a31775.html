"Manacle" is a chilling and immersive horror game that will keep you on the edge of your seat. The game is set in an impoverished town where the inhabitants resort to dark and dangerous means in order to survive. The people who live there are haunted by their own desperation.



Discover the truth
Manacle puts you in the shoes of a desperate father struggling to survive in a world of poverty. Forced to become a doctor in the dangerous and illegal world of the black market. But an accident changes his destiny forever.

Are you brave enough to face the horrors of Manacle? Will you uncover the secrets behind the town's decline and discover the root of its evil? Get ready to experience the ultimate survival horror game and enter the world of Manacle".





-Menacle is a solo developed by one person
-First-person with a spine-chilling atmosphere
-Immersive gameplay and puzzle-solving experience
-Eastern cultural references
-Unique and appealing graphics
-English and Chinese supported
